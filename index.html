P Privacy Policy Prompt-Ducer — From Idea To Creation Effective: February 24, 2026 Overview Prompt-Ducer ("the App") is developed by TrevBeats Multimedia. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. The App is designed to run with minimal data exposure. Information We Do Not Collect Prompt-Ducer does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following: Your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information Account credentials or authentication data Location data Device identifiers beyond standard iOS system identifiers Photos, contacts, calendar data, or any on-device content Health or biometric data AI Prompt Generation When you use the AI generation feature, your prompt topic or idea is sent to our secure cloud proxy (Firebase Cloud Functions), which forwards the request to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. Your input text is transmitted securely over HTTPS We do not log or store the content of your prompts on our servers Anthropic may process your input in accordance with their own Privacy Policy Your Anthropic API key is never stored on your device — all API calls are proxied through our secure server Local Data Storage Prompt history and saved prompts are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data: Never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it Is not backed up to our servers Can be deleted at any time by deleting the App Voice Input (Speech Recognition) The App includes an optional voice input feature powered by Apple's Speech Recognition framework. If you use this feature: Audio is processed by Apple's on-device or server-based speech recognition We do not receive, store, or transmit your voice data Apple's use of this data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy Third-Party Services The App uses the following third-party services: Anthropic Claude API — AI prompt generation. Privacy Policy Google Firebase — Secure API proxy. Privacy Policy Apple Speech Framework — Optional voice input.
